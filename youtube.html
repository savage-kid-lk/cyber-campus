Make Money on YouTube

Here are 15 ways to monetize your YouTube presence:

1. AdSense

Earn money from ads displayed on your videos.

  • Enable monetization on your YouTube account
  • Meet YouTube's monetization requirements (1,000 subscribers, 4,000 watch hours)
  • Earn R10-R50 per 1,000 views
  • Average YouTube AdSense earnings in South Africa: R5,000-R20,000 per month

2. Sponsorships

Partner with brands to promote their products/services in your videos.

  • Identify brands aligned with your niche
  • Reach out to brands for sponsorship opportunities
  • Create sponsored content (product placements, reviews)
  • Earn R5,000-R50,000 per sponsored video
  • Popular sponsorship platforms:
    • AspireIQ
    • Grin
    • Upfluence

3. Affiliate Marketing

Earn commissions by promoting products/services in your videos.

  • Join affiliate programs (Takealot, Amazon Associates)
  • Share affiliate links in video descriptions
  • Earn R500-R5,000 per sale
  • Popular affiliate programs in South Africa:
    • Takealot Affiliate Program
    • Amazon Associates

4. Merchandise

Sell branded merchandise (t-shirts, hats, etc.) to your audience.

  • Design merchandise using online tools (Teespring, Redbubble)
  • Sell merchandise through YouTube merchandise feature
  • Earn R100-R1,000 per sale
  • Popular merchandise platforms:
    • Teespring
    • Redbubble
    • Shopify

5. Premium Content

Offer exclusive content (videos, podcasts) for paid subscribers.

  • Use YouTube Memberships or Patreon
  • Create exclusive content for paid subscribers
  • Earn R100-R1,000 per month per subscriber
  • Popular premium content platforms:
    • YouTube Memberships
    • Patreon

6. YouTube Premium Revenue

Earn a share of YouTube Premium revenue based on watch time.

  • Meet YouTube Premium requirements
  • Earn R10-R50 per 1,000 views
  • Average YouTube Premium earnings in South Africa: R5,000-R20,000 per month

7. Licensing

Licence your content to other media companies or platforms.

  • Identify licensing opportunities
  • Negotiate licensing deals
  • Earn R5,000-R50,000 per licensing deal
  • Popular licensing platforms:
    • Jukin Media
    • Viralty

8. YouTube Super Chat

Earn money from live streaming through Super Chat.

  • Enable Super Chat on your YouTube account
  • Earn R10-R100 per Super Chat message
  • Average YouTube Super Chat earnings in South Africa: R1,000-R5,000 per month

9. YouTube Memberships

Earn money from channel memberships.

  • Enable channel memberships on your YouTube account
  • Earn R10-R100 per month per member
  • Average YouTube membership earnings in South Africa: R1,000-R5,000 per month

    Earn money from live streaming on YouTube.

    • Enable live streaming on your YouTube account

    Create and sell online courses on YouTube.

    • Create high-quality course content
    • Utilize YouTube's course creation features
    • Sell courses through YouTube or external platforms
    • Earn R1,000-R10,000 per course sale
    • Popular course platforms:
      • Udemy
      • Teachable
      • Skillshare

    13. Consulting

    Offer consulting services to individuals or businesses.

    • Identify your expertise
    • Reach out to potential clients
    • Offer consulting services (video calls, meetings)
    • Earn R1,000-R10,000 per hour
    • Popular consulting platforms:
      • Clarity
      • Zoom
      • Skype

    • Earn R50,000-R200,000 per original content series

      • YouTube Premium
