Make Money on TikTok

1. Ad Revenue

Earn money from ads displayed on your videos.

  • Enable monetization on your TikTok account
  • Meet TikTok's monetization requirements (1,000 followers, 10,000 views)
  • Earn R10-R50 per 1,000 views
  • Average TikTok ad revenue in South Africa: R5,000-R20,000 per month

2. Sponsored Content

Partner with brands to promote their products/services.

  • Identify brands aligned with your niche
  • Reach out to brands for sponsorship opportunities
  • Create sponsored content (videos, hashtags)
  • Earn R5,000-R50,000 per sponsored post
  • Popular sponsorship platforms:
    • AspireIQ
    • Grin
    • Upfluence

3. Affiliate Marketing

Earn commissions by promoting products/services.

  • Join affiliate programs (Takealot, Amazon Associates)
  • Share affiliate links in bio or through affiliate platforms
  • Earn R500-R5,000 per sale
  • Popular affiliate programs in South Africa:
    • Takealot Affiliate Program
    • Amazon Associates

4. Selling Merchandise

Sell branded merchandise to your audience.

  • Design and create merchandise (t-shirts, hats, etc.)
  • Sell merchandise through TikTok or external platforms
  • Earn R500-R5,000 per sale
  • Popular merchandise platforms:
    • Redbubble
    • Teespring
    • Printful

5. TikTok Live Streaming

Earn money from live streaming.

  • Utilize TikTok Live for live streaming
  • Earn R100-R1,000 per live stream
  • Average TikTok Live earnings in South Africa: R5,000-R20,000 per month
