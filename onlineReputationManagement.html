Online Reputation Management (ORM) Strategies

1. Monitoring

  • Google Alerts:
    • Free service offered by Google
    • Receive email notifications for new content matching search queries
    • Set up alerts for brand mentions, competitors, and industry-related keywords
    • Visit Google Alerts (https://www.google.com/alerts)
    • How to set up Google Alerts:
      1. Go to Google Alerts
      2. Enter your search query
      3. Select the type of alert (News, Blogs, Video, Discussions, or Everything)
      4. Choose the frequency of alerts (As-it-happens, Daily, or Weekly)
      5. Enter your email address
      6. Click "Create Alert"
  • Social Media Listening Tools:
    • Monitor social media platforms for brand mentions and keywords
    • Examples: Hootsuite (R500/month), Sprout Social (R1,500/month), Brand24 (R500/month)
    • Set up keywords and brand mentions
    • Monitor social media conversations
    • Respond to customer inquiries
    • Analyze sentiment analysis
    • Benefits:
      • Improved customer engagement
      • Enhanced brand awareness
      • Competitor analysis
      • Identify industry trends
  • Review Tracking:
    • Monitor online review platforms for customer feedback
    • Examples: Google My Business, Yelp, TripAdvisor, Facebook Reviews
    • Claim your business listings
    • Set up review notifications
    • Respond to customer reviews
    • Analyze review sentiment
    • Benefits:
      • Improved online reputation
      • Increased customer trust
      • Enhanced brand awareness
      • Identify areas for improvement
